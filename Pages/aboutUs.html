ABOUT US

When friends Rob and Kev decided that they were tired of trawling through endless guitar and music stores to find what they were looking for, they started to form an idea - "Why not establish a place where musicians can easily find what they need or want?" Being musicians themselves, and having played together since childhood, they decided to take matters into them own hands and make it so!

So, in late 2022, in London, England, our guitar store was founded. Born out of a desire to easily provide the best quality instruments and only essential accessories to meet every budget. Without any unnecessary extras. From high quality beginner or budget guitars, to the super premium high-end masterpieces that are comparable to those used by international touring musicians, we aim to provide you with what you are looking for.

By working closely with suppliers and ensuring we are able to procure our instruments and accessories direct from the suppliers wherever possible we are able to cut down on logistic fees. This allows us to pass savings onto you (Cliche alert). Although we have not been around for that long, we can promise you that you will receive the best quality items and the best quality service from us.

This isn't just a store, it is a way of life, as Kev frequently repeats Keith Richards famous quote - Music is a necessity. After food, air, water and warmth, music is the next necessity of life. Truly words to live by, Which we do. Every day.
